Frequently Asked Questions
Which AC brand is best for Chennai's climate?

Chennai's hot and coastal humid climate demands high-efficiency, corrosion-resistant cooling. We highly recommend Daikin and Mitsubishi for their superior, silent inverter technology, or Blue Star for excellent fast cooling. Visit our showroom, and we'll guide you based on your specific room size and budget.

Do all your ACs have 100% copper coils?

Absolutely. CRS Aircon strictly only sells air conditioners with 100% copper condenser coils. Copper transfers heat faster, is vastly more durable, and is highly corrosion-resistant — making it critical for Chennai's humid, salty air.

What exactly does the ₹999 AMC include?

Our Annual Maintenance Contract includes two comprehensive preventive wet services, priority response for breakdown support calls, thorough gas pressure checks, electrical health checks, and deep chemical cleaning of filters and coils.

How fast is installation after I buy?

We pride ourselves on speed. CRS Aircon completes most AC installations within 24 hours of delivery. Our trained in-house team handles all aspects including copper piping, gas charging, core cutting, electrical setup, and a full test run.

Are you an authorized dealer or a reseller?

We are a direct, factory-authorized dealer for all 8 brands we carry, including Daikin, Blue Star, Carrier, Mitsubishi, Voltas, Hitachi, Godrej, and LG. Buying from an authorized dealer ensures you receive 100% genuine units, official manufacturer warranties, and authorized after-sales service.
