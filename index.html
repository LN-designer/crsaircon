Which AC brand is best for Chennai's climate?

Chennai's hot and humid climate needs high-efficiency cooling. We recommend Daikin and Mitsubishi for inverter tech, or Blue Star for dehumidification. Visit us — we'll help based on room size and budget.

Do all your ACs have copper coils?

Yes — 100% copper condenser coils only. Copper is more efficient, durable, and corrosion-resistant — critical for Chennai's coastal humidity.
